The Best Movies According to Joey

My top three movies of all-time.

The Lord of the Rings Trilogy

Is it cheating to include a trilogy as one entry? I don't think so! These characters mean so much to me, this series changed literature and fantasy, and the story reminds me to never lose hope.

Kubo and the Two Strings

This is a beautiful and delightful stop-motion animated movie that I return to time and again when I need some magic and joy.

Men in Black

I grew up watching this movie over and over. It is fun, it has aliens, and it has been a staple in my life since I was a child.
